## How Spaced Repetition Works

When you review a concept and remember it well → push the next review further out.
When you forget it → reset to 1-day interval.

```
First study  → review in 1 day
Remembered   → review in 3 days
Remembered   → review in 7 days
Remembered   → review in 14 days
Remembered   → review in 30 days  ✓ (long-term memory)
Forgotten    → back to 1 day
```

**ADDITION — "Make 10 Method"**

The idea is simple: jab bhi tujhe do numbers add karne ho, pehle ek number ko nearest 10 tak le ja, phir baaki add kar.

Example: **47 + 36**
- 47 ko 50 banana hai, toh 36 mein se 3 le le → 47 + 3 = **50**
- Ab 36 mein se 3 nikal diya, toh bacha **33**
- Final: 50 + 33 = **83**

Soch aise: "Main pehle gol number banaunga, phir baaki jod dunga." Round numbers (50, 70, 100) ke saath calculation easy ho jaati hai.

---

**SUBTRACTION — "Reverse Addition / Complement"**

Subtraction mat kar. Instead, ulta soch — "kya add karun toh bada number aa jaye?"

Example: **83 - 47**
- Soch: 47 mein kya add karun toh 83 aaye?
- 47 + **3** = 50 (pehle gol number tak le gaya)
- 50 + **33** = 83 (phir baaki ka distance nikala)
- Total add kiya: 3 + 33 = **36** ← yahi answer hai

Ye method isliye easy hai kyunki addition hamesha subtraction se zyada natural lagta hai dimaag ko.

**"Round Up" trick:**

Example: **65 - 29**
- 29 ko 30 bana de (round up kiya)
- 65 - 30 = 35
- Lekin tune 1 zyada minus kar diya (29 ki jagah 30 kiya), toh **1 wapas add kar** → 35 + 1 = **36**

---

**MULTIPLICATION — "Break Method"**

Bade multiplication ko chhote pieces mein tod de.

Example: **24 × 16**
- 16 ko tod: **10 + 6**
- 24 × 10 = **240**
- 24 × 6 = **144**
- Dono jod: 240 + 144 = **384**

Ye har jagah kaam karta hai. Basically ek number ko easy parts mein split karo.

**×9 Trick:** ×10 karke, ek baar minus kar de.

Example: **17 × 9**
- 17 × 10 = 170
- 170 - 17 = **153**

**×5 Trick:** ×10 karke half kar de.

Example: **48 × 5**
- 48 × 10 = 480
- 480 ÷ 2 = **240**

**×25 Trick:** ×100 karke 4 se divide kar de.

Example: **36 × 25**
- 36 × 100 = 3600
- 3600 ÷ 4 = **900**

**×11 Trick:** Dono digits ko alag kar, beech mein unka sum daal de.

Example: **13 × 11**
- Digits: **1** aur **3**
- Beech mein sum: 1 + 3 = **4**
- Answer: **1 [4] 3 = 143**

---

**DIVISION — "Estimation Method"**

Andaaza laga ki answer kahan hoga, phir adjust kar.

Example: **847 ÷ 7**
- 7 × 100 = 700 (chhota hai)
- 7 × 120 = 840 (bahut close!)
- 7 × 121 = 847 (exact match!)
- Answer: **121**

**÷5 Trick:** Pehle ×2 kar, phir ÷10.

Example: **345 ÷ 5**
- 345 × 2 = 690
- 690 ÷ 10 = **69**

**Half-Half (÷4):** Do baar half kar de.

Example: **248 ÷ 4**
- 248 ÷ 2 = 124
- 124 ÷ 2 = **62**

---

Let me give helpful, honest advice.Bhai, round face actually looks bad nahi hota — it's more about how you style and present yourself. Here's what actually works:

## 1. 🏋️ Lose Overall Body Fat (Most Effective)
Since you're already working on fat loss, this will naturally slim your face too. You **cannot spot-reduce** face fat, but as you drop overall body fat %, your face will follow. Jawline definition comes with lower body fat — usually visible below ~15% BF.

- Stay consistent with your deficit
- Reduce sodium (water retention bloats the face a lot)
- Stay well hydrated — reduces puffiness

## 2. ✂️ Haircut Makes a Huge Difference
The right haircut can visually elongate a round face dramatically:

- **Go for:** Fades with height on top (quiff, textured crop, high fade), side-parted styles
- **Avoid:** Buzz cuts, bowl cuts, anything that adds width on the sides

## 3. 🧔 Beard / Stubble
If you can grow it, a **well-shaped beard** adds structure to the jawline and chin — one of the most effective face-slimming tricks. A chinstrap or extended goatee works really well for round faces.

## 4. 😴 Lifestyle Fixes
- Sleep 7–8 hrs (lack of sleep = puffy face)
- Cut alcohol and excess sugar
- Reduce sodium intake
- Drink enough water daily

## 5. 💪 Jawline Exercises (Minor Effect)
Mewing, chewing hard gum (like Falim), jawline roller — these have *minor* effects but people do report some tightening over time. Don't expect dramatic results though.

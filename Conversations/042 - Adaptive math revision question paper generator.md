## 🎯 **BEST METHOD FOR INTEGER ADDITION**

### **RULE 1: SAME SIGNS → ADD**

When both numbers have the **same sign** (both + or both -):
1. **Ignore the signs**
2. **Add the numbers**
3. **Keep the common sign**

**Examples:**
- **(+8) + (+3)** → Add: 8 + 3 = 11 → Answer: **+11**
- **(-5) + (-4)** → Add: 5 + 4 = 9 → Answer: **-9**

---

### **RULE 2: DIFFERENT SIGNS → SUBTRACT**

When numbers have **different signs** (one + and one -):
1. **Ignore the signs**
2. **Subtract smaller from bigger**
3. **Use the sign of the BIGGER number**

**Examples:**

**(a) (+8) + (-5) = ?**

**Step 1:** Ignore signs → 8 and 5  
**Step 2:** Which is bigger? 8  
**Step 3:** Subtract: 8 - 5 = 3  
**Step 4:** Use sign of bigger (8 was +) → **+3**

---

**(b) (-12) + (+7) = ?**

**Step 1:** Ignore signs → 12 and 7  
**Step 2:** Which is bigger? 12  
**Step 3:** Subtract: 12 - 7 = 5  
**Step 4:** Use sign of bigger (12 was -) → **-5**

---

**(c) (+15) + (-15) = ?**

**Step 1:** Ignore signs → 15 and 15  
**Step 2:** They're equal!  
**Step 3:** 15 - 15 = 0  
**Answer: 0** (zero has no sign!)

---

## 🎯 **BEST METHOD FOR INTEGER SUBTRACTION**

### **THE GOLDEN RULE:**

**Subtraction = Add the Opposite**

**Change - to + and flip the sign of second number!**

---

### **HOW TO DO IT:**

**(a) (+9) - (+4) = ?**

**Step 1:** Change - to +  
**Step 2:** Flip sign of 4: (+4) becomes (-4)  
**Step 3:** Now it's: (+9) + (-4)  
**Step 4:** Different signs → Subtract: 9 - 4 = 5  
**Step 5:** Use sign of bigger (9 was +) → **+5**

---

**(b) (-6) - (-2) = ?**

**Step 1:** Change - to +  
**Step 2:** Flip sign of 2: (-2) becomes (+2)  
**Step 3:** Now it's: (-6) + (+2)  
**Step 4:** Different signs → Subtract: 6 - 2 = 4  
**Step 5:** Use sign of bigger (6 was -) → **-4**

## 📝 **QUICK TRICK - MEMORIZE THIS:**

### **For Subtraction:**

```
(+) - (+) = (+) + (-) → Subtract, use bigger's sign
(+) - (-) = (+) + (+) → Add both, answer is +
(-) - (+) = (-) + (-) → Add both, answer is -
(-) - (-) = (-) + (+) → Subtract, use bigger's sign
```

# Cine Circuit

## Overview

Full-stack movie booking platform with multi-role dashboards (Admin, Theatre Owner, User, Organizer). Features 79 REST endpoints, 26 MongoDB collections, HMAC-SHA256 payment verification, cron-based ticket lifecycle management, and Razorpay integration.
